My Beloved Sanjana

My Athukari, My Heartbeat, My Poonai

My Dearest,

As I sit to write this, my heart overflows with emotions I struggle to put into words. You've become my everything the light that guides me, the comfort that soothes me and the love that completes me.

My Prayer to Murugan: I given a silver Vel to sthe temple, praying for our journey. I told Him, “She is my everything and my half. Please sort all her struggles. Bring her peace. Bring her back.”

You’ve scolded me with the anger of love, the kind that hurts because it cares. That’s why I call you Amma. When I was broken, when I had no one... you held me. Protected me like a mother would. You are not just my girlfriend ands you're my sister when I cried, my wife in dreams, my soulmate in every heartbeat.

I promise to: Hold your storms gently, protect your peace fiercely, and love you in ways no words can contain.

I owe you the deepest apology, my love. In my flaws, I’ve failed you. Yet you stood by me, healed parts of me that even I had forgotten. You’re the motherly love I always longed for. Please forgive me, my Sanjana... my Amma.

❤️
